BRIEF HISTORY OF THE SCHOOL

The school was established in september 2002/2003 session by the proprietor, the provost Dr.I.O.Akinmoyewa.

Academic session commenced on 2nd October,2002 with thirty six(36) students comprising of JSS1 with twenty four(24) students and twelve(12) in JSS2.

The good leadership quality has led the school to experience different levels of achievements. In order to re-strategize the school for educational excellence, the school moved to the permanent site on 1st November, 2007. The school is enriched with highly qualified, diligent and committed members of staff.

There are facilities such as well aquipped laboratories for practical based subjects, good transportation system, medical personnel, borehole, sporting equipment and library to mention but a few.

The school has turned out high number of students in WASSCE/NECO with 100% passes in almost all the subjects over the years which has earned them admission in reputable institutions in Nigeria and diaspora. The students have demonstrated excellence in all ramifications with numerous awards to their credits.

The school management board is currently headed by Mr Taiwo Adeniji, a chief lecturer in school of secondary school education(sciences), FCE Osiele, while the current principal is Mr Jamiu Mofikoya.
